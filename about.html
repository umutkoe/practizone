About Practizone

More Than a Platform. A 100% Canadian Dental Community.

We are committed to serving the dental community with excellence, reliability, and a people-first approach to staffing and practice support.

Our Core Values

Mission

Bring energy and innovation to dental practices across Canada by closing staffing gaps and supporting growth.

Empowerment

Enable clinics to stay fully focused on patient care without operational staffing pressure.

Excellence

Deliver exceptional quality in staffing services, practice tools, and continued support.

Expansion

Scale across provinces while staying connected to local community values and clinic needs.

Education

Contribute to ongoing industry development through practical learning and upskilling pathways.

Engagement

Build meaningful relationships and customized solutions that create long-term mutual growth.

Founder and CEO: Eva Ribeiro

With over 20 years of hands-on experience in the dental industry, Eva brings strategic leadership, operational expertise, and deep understanding of patient-centered practice growth.

She completed the Dental Management Certificate (DMC) at the University of Toronto (2008-2009) and continues advanced education through the 2023-2024 program cycle.

Her approach balances the business side of dentistry with care-focused excellence, helping clinics improve both operations and outcomes.
