UI Design Rules (Non-Negotiable)
- Do NOT use glassmorphism, neumorphism, claymorphism, frosted blur cards, or overly decorative trendy UI effects.
- Keep the interface modern, clean, and professional. Avoid exaggerated visuals.
- Use simple depth: subtle shadows, clear spacing, strong typography hierarchy, and clean sections.
- Keep border radius moderate (not overly rounded), and avoid noisy gradients.
- Focus on readability, trust, and medical-corporate clarity.
- Use only project images from `practizone-img/` and keep image treatments natural (no heavy filters).
- Reuse logo colors as the primary palette and keep contrast accessible.
- Animations should be minimal and purposeful (soft fade/slide only).
- Layout should be responsive on desktop and mobile without visual clutter.
